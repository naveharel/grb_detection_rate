Settings
Optical survey mode

Requires вүҘ i detections in one night (intra-night) or a single detection per night across nights (inter-night). Activates tnight and defines fnight = tnight/24 h; sub-night (intra-night) cadences are rescaled by fnight, per-day (inter-night) cadences are unchanged. Requires fnight вүҘ flive.

tOH approximation

Drops tOH from the exposure formula to enable closed-form optimal strategies, while still enforcing texp > tOH as the validity boundary.

Color by detection regime

Tints the surface by dominant regime: warm = flux-limited (DEuc), cool = cadence-limited (Di), gray = singly-limited (Ddec). Brighter = higher range.

Full integral mode

Integrates Rdet over all viewing angles q вҲҲ [0, qnr] (thesis Eq. 39) instead of using the dominant-term approximation. Slower but more accurate.
